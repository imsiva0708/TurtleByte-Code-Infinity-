Full Week Workout Plans

Disclaimer: These are sample plans and should be adjusted based on individual fitness levels, goals, and any medical limitations. Always consult a doctor or certified personal trainer before starting a new exercise program.

Beginner:

  • Monday: Full-body strength training + 30 minutes brisk walking
  • Tuesday: Rest or gentle yoga/stretching
  • Wednesday: Cardio + core exercises
  • Thursday: Rest
  • Friday: Full-body strength training + low-impact cardio
  • Weekend: Rest or active recovery

Intermediate:

  • Monday: Chest & Triceps + HIIT cardio
  • Tuesday: Back & Biceps + moderate-intensity cardio
  • Wednesday: Rest or active recovery
  • Thursday: Legs & Core + moderate-intensity cardio
  • Friday: Shoulders & Core + low-impact cardio
  • Weekend: Rest or active recovery

Advanced:

  • Monday: Push + HIIT cardio
  • Tuesday: Pull + moderate-intensity cardio
  • Wednesday: Rest or active recovery
  • Thursday: Legs + moderate-intensity cardio
  • Friday: Upper Body + low-impact cardio or core work
  • Saturday & Sunday: Rest or active recovery
